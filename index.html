Your Empire quests

How would you represent yourself as a Solflare Guardian?

+Reveal challenge

Write one short Guardian pledge in your own words.

+Reveal challenge

The Code

Quest rules

Keep it clean, original, and easy to review.

  • One submissionEach applicant may submit only once.
  • Original workAll responses must be entirely your own.
  • No spamLow-effort, copied, or irrelevant entries will be rejected.
  • Complete answersAll five quest questions must be answered.
  • Respect the formatFollow the prompt instructions and keep responses readable.
The Rubric

How you will be judged

Transparent scoring makes the quest feel fair and serious.

  • Value to Solflare How useful you are to the Empire and its community goals.
    30%
  • Originality Whether your answers feel fresh, thoughtful, and genuine.
    25%
  • Clarity How clearly and efficiently you communicate your ideas.
    20%
  • Alignment How well you reflect Solflare's mission and community style.
    15%
  • Presentation Confidence, structure, and overall polish of your submission.
    10%
